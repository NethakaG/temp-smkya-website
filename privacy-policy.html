Privacy Policy

This Policy explains how SMKYA collects, uses, and protects information connected to the platform and services.

Updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy is issued by Samaakhya Private Limited, trading as SMKYA / smkya.com. This Policy discloses the privacy practices for the smkya.com Platform, any related apps or software, and any social media content made available by smkya.com (collectively, the "Platform"), as well as related products and services we may offer to you (collectively, the "Services").

This Policy also covers how personal and other information that we receive or collect about you is treated. You acknowledge that this Privacy Policy is designed to be read in connection with the Platform Terms and Conditions of Use, and that by accessing or using the Platform, you agree to be bound by the Platform's terms and conditions, as well as this Policy.

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We may notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account, placing a prominent notice on our Platform, and updating privacy information on this page.

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us at info@smkya.com, smkyalife@gmail.com, or +94 72 149 9479.

Important: By using the Platform and/or offerings available on this Platform, you give your consent that all personal data you submit may be processed by us in the manner and for the purposes described below. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, do not use the Platform.

1. Types of Information We Collect

In order to better provide you with fitness plans, fitness videos, life coaching, and meal plans, we collect two types of information about our users: Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") and Aggregate Information.

Personally Identifiable Information

This refers to information that lets us know the specifics of who you are. When you engage in certain activities on this Platform or any platform used to provide the services, such as registering for an account, downloading or purchasing a product or service, submitting content, posting content in user forums, or sending feedback, we may ask you to provide certain information about yourself.

Examples of PII may include your first and last name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, and other identifying information. When ordering products or services on the Platform, you may be asked to provide a credit card number.

SMKYA may also collect sensitive wellness information where relevant to the services, including height, weight, age, photos, progress photos, measurements, nutrition logs, food preferences, allergies, injuries, medical conditions, medication information, menstrual-cycle information, pregnancy/postpartum status, mental-health related information, sleep, stress, and lifestyle information.

Aggregate Information

This refers to information that does not by itself identify a specific individual. We gather certain information based upon where you visit on our Platform and what other websites may have directed you to us. This information may include the website URL that points to the Platform, which URL you go to after visiting our Platform, what browser you are using, and your Internet Protocol ("IP") address.

2. How We Collect and Use Information

We do not collect PII about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. However, you may be required to provide certain PII when you elect to use certain products or services available on the Platform. These may include registering for an account, entering a contest, signing up for offers, sending an email message, submitting a form, transmitting information by telephone or letter, or submitting payment information when purchasing products and services.

When processing certain information, such as payment information with affiliated banking institutions or payment processors, we may encrypt the transaction using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology to help prevent PII from being stolen or intercepted.

We primarily use your PII to provide product or service offerings to you. We may also use PII to enhance Platform operations, improve internal marketing and promotional efforts, statistically analyze Platform use, improve product and service offerings, customize Platform content, contact you regarding administrative notices, resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, and enforce agreements including the Platform Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy.

We may also collect, or our third party advertising partners may collect, certain Aggregate Information. For example, we may use your IP address to diagnose problems with servers or software, administer the Platform, and gather demographic information.

3. Cookies

Depending on how you use our Platform, we may store cookies on your computer in order to collect certain aggregate data about users and customize aspects of your experience. A cookie is a small data text file stored on your computer that uniquely identifies your browser.

Cookies may include information such as your IP address, browser type, the server your computer is logged onto, the area code and zip code associated with your server, and your first name to welcome you back to the Platform. We may use cookies to monitor aggregate Platform usage metrics, store and remember passwords if you allow us to do so, store account and advertising preferences, and personalize Services. We do not use cookies to track your browsing behaviors.

Most browsers initially accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. Some aspects of the Platform may not function properly if you disable cookies.

4. Release of Information

We will not sell, trade, or rent your PII to others. We provide some product and service offerings through contractual arrangements with affiliates, service providers, partners, and other third parties ("Service Partners"). We and our Service Partners may need to use some PII in order to perform tasks, deliver products or services, confirm payment, provide customer service, or otherwise support services you request.

We encourage Service Partners to adopt and promote strong privacy policies. However, the use of your PII by Service Partners is governed by their respective privacy policies and is not subject to our control. Other Platforms accessible through this Platform have their own privacy policies and data collection, use, and disclosure practices. We are not responsible for the policies or practices of third parties.

Occasionally we may be required by law enforcement or judicial authorities to provide PII to appropriate governmental authorities. In such cases, we may disclose PII upon receipt of a court order, subpoena, or to cooperate with a law enforcement investigation. We reserve the right to report to law enforcement agencies any activities that we in good faith believe to be unlawful.

We may also provide Aggregate Information about customer sales, traffic patterns, and related Platform information to third party advertisers, but these statistics do not include Personally Identifiable Information.

5. User Choices on Collection and Use of Information

We may send you email regarding new products and services that we feel may interest you. If you indicated that you are interested in receiving offers or information from us and our partners, we may occasionally send direct or electronic mail about products and services that may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive solicitations, you can opt out by editing your account information or contacting us.

You also have choices with respect to cookies. By modifying browser preferences, you can accept all cookies, be notified when a cookie is set, or reject all cookies. If you reject all cookies, parts of the Platform may not work properly.

6. Miscellaneous

You must be at least 18 years old to have our permission to use this Platform. If you are below the age of 18, a parent or guardian must register on behalf of the minor to use this Platform. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose PII about minor visitors.

You should be aware that when Personally Identifiable Information is voluntarily disclosed in discussion forums or other public areas on this Platform, that information can be collected and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from third parties. Such activities are beyond our control and this Policy does not apply to such information.

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries about our Privacy Policy or privacy practices, please contact us at info@smkya.com or smkyalife@gmail.com.
