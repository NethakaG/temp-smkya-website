Life Coaching

Most programmes tell you what to do. Life coaching at SMKYA helps you understand why you do what you do and how to change it.

We support individuals navigating stress, low confidence, loneliness, burnout, life transitions, or those feeling disconnected from themselves. The focus is mindset, vision, and sustainable personal growth; helping you reflect, realign, build confidence, and move forward with clarity.

What is included:

  • Weekly life coaching sessions
  • Mindset and vision check-ins to realign goals and purpose
  • Personalised growth action plans
  • Voice note and SMS support
  • Progress & milestone reviews to reflect, recalibrate, and celebrate wins

Who this is for:

Anyone navigating stress, burnout, low confidence, or a life transition. Anyone who keeps getting in their own way despite knowing what they want.

Fitness Coaching

Your workout programme is designed around your body, lifestyle, schedule, training history, injuries, goals, and emotional capacity. The focus is functional strength, sustainable physical health, and helping you build strength, confidence, energy, and trust in what your body can do.

What is included:

  • Initial consultation
  • Weekly one-on-one sessions
  • Personalised workout plan
  • Personalised lifestyle guidance
  • Progress tracking
  • SMS and email support

Who this is for:

Anyone who wants to build real physical strength and trust in their body, whether you are just starting, returning after a break, or looking to train with more intention.

Nutritional Guidance

This is practical and sustainable nutrition support for people who want to eat with more structure and confidence, without cutting out the foods that are part of their life and culture, and without the cycle of restriction and guilt. Your guidance is built around your lifestyle, your preferences, your stress levels, and your long-term energy.

What is included:

  • Nutrition guidance tailored to your lifestyle, routine, and preferences
  • Flexible approach that accounts for cultural food norms, social eating, and busy schedules
  • Education on what your body needs and why
  • Focus on sustainable habits, not short-term results

Oshara

Dietitian & Clinical Nutritionist

For clients who need more medically informed nutrition support, SMKYA may involve Oshara, whose expertise allows for a deeper understanding of nutrition in relation to medical needs, absorption, and health related factors.

Who this is for:

Anyone who feels stuck in cycles of restrictive eating, or who simply wants to eat in a way that actually matches how they want to feel and function.
