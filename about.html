About Samaakhya Gajanaayke

The Coach Behind SMKYA

A decade of training, an athlete's discipline, and a deeply human approach to wellness.

Her Story

From International Athlete to Holistic Coach

Samaakhya Gajanaayke spent over a decade competing at international level in sport. Through that journey she discovered something most fitness culture ignores: the body and mind are not separate. Peak performance demanded mental resilience, nutritional awareness, and emotional intelligence - not just physical training.

When she founded SMKYA, she brought all of that together. Her approach isn't a program - it's a philosophy. One built around the belief that real, lasting change only happens when the whole person is coached: their movement, their nourishment, and their mindset.

Today, Samaakhya coaches clients online from around the world - from Sri Lanka to Toronto - helping them break free from crash diets, inconsistent routines, and the cycle of starting over. Every program she builds is entirely personalised. Nothing is templated. Nothing is rushed.

Her Approach

Three pillars. One integrated method.

🏋️

Movement Science

Training that respects your body's history, capacity, and goals. Functional. Sustainable. Yours.

🥗

Nourishment

Nutrition that fits your culture, lifestyle and stress load. No restriction. No guilt.

🧠

Mindset

Confidence, clarity, and inner strength that outlast any fitness phase.
