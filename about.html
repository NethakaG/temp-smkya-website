The Person
Behind SMKYA

Samaakhya Gajanaayke built this practice on a single observation: physical transformation alone does not make a person's life better.

Background

Where the approach came from

After more than a decade of coaching and competing at an international level, Samaakhya had worked with enough people to see a pattern. Clients who followed the plan, hit the numbers, and looked the part but still felt stuck, inconsistent, or unfulfilled vs. clients who made slower, quieter progress but built something that lasted.

The difference was never the workout.

That insight is what SMKYA is built on. Samaakhya holds a Level 5 qualification in psychology and has studied where life coaching and behavioural understanding meet fitness. She is a coach who takes the mindset side of transformation as seriously as the physical side, because experience has shown her that one without the other rarely works.

"I found that people who struggled most were not failing at fitness. They were working toward a goal they had never actually chosen for themselves."

Samaakhya Gajanayake

Founder of SMKYA
